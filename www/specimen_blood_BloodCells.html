Blood Cells

Did you know that...

A blood cell, also called a haematocyte, is a cell
produced by haematopoiesis and normally found in blood.



There are 3 blood cell types:

  • Red blood cells (erythrocytes)
  • White blood cells (Leukocytes)
  • Platelets (thrombocytes).
