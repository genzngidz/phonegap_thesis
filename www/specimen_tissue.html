Tissue

  • Epithelial Tissue

    covers the body surface and forms the
    lining for most internal cavities. The
    major function of epithelial tissue includes
    protection, secretion, absorption, and filtration.

  • Connective Tissue

    is the most abundant and the most widely distributed
    of the tissues. Connective tissues perform a variety
    of functions including support and protection.

  • Nerve Tissue

    composed of specialized cells which not only
    receive stimuli but also conduct impulses to
    and from all parts of the body. Nerve cells or
    neurons are long and string-like.
