Nerve Tissue

Did you know that...

It is composed of specialized cells which not only receive stimuli but also conduct impulses to and from all parts of the body. Nerve cells or neurons are long and string-like.

three main types of neurons:

  • Sensory (or afferent) Neurons, conduct impulses from the sensory organs to the central nervous system (brain and spinal cord)
  • Motor (or efferent) Neurons, conduct impulses from the central nervous system to the effector organs (such as muscles and glands)
  • . Interneurons , (also known as connector neurons or association neurons) are those that connect sensory neurons to motor neurons
